Tips & Tricks

1

  Strategies


Over the ages, sudoku solvers have amassed a wide variety of tips & strategies to solve sudoku puzzles more quickly. This page presents some of the more common strategies used today.

2

  Difficulty Levels


The tips presented are roughly ordered in terms of complexity, with less complex strategies appearing first. The advanced strategies should be reserved only for the most difficult puzzles.

3

  Practice!!


The only way to get better is to practice! After learning each strategy, try your hand at a sudoku puzzle and look for ways to use your newfound knowledge. Happy solving!!
